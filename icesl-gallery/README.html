Superquadrics

Defined by implicit surfaces that are superquadrics, ie ${distanceEstimator}({pt}, radius, p) = (abs({pt})^p)^\frac{1}{p} - radius$

For details, read appendix of Sphere tracing: a geometric method for the antialiased ray tracing of implicit surfaces, John C. Hart
